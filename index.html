Regular affirmations suggest that you should deceive yourself (I am the most beautiful, I am the best).

But as soon as we start deceiving ourselves, we stop believing in ourselves.

It's much better to understand and ask yourself: "What is the unconscious benefit of this?

What is Neome?
Neome is a mobile app that helps build confidence, overcome fears and set goals.

We provide personalised techniques and exercises to help you understand and overcome doubts, develop confidence and create effective plans to achieve goals.
How does AI Neome work?
AI Neome uses a combination of artificial intelligence, personalised exercises and data analysis to help users understand their doubts, overcome fears and develop confidence. The app tailors its content to individual users, helping them identify and challenge limiting beliefs while providing actionable plans to achieve their goals. By continuously adapting and refining its approach, AI Neome effectively guides users towards personal growth and positive change.
What is a personal program?
A personal programme in Neome is a tailor-made plan designed specifically for you. It takes into account your unique goals, challenges and experiences.

It consists of personalised exercises and methods to help you overcome doubts, fears and limiting beliefs.

It is created using artificial intelligence to ensure that it meets your individual needs and goals, making it a powerful tool to support your personal growth and development journey.
